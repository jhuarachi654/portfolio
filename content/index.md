## FAQs


<!-- section: Text wrapper -->

Who am I?

I am a multidisciplinary product designer and a lifelong learner. I am currently finishing my Masters in Interaction Design at California College of the Arts, graduating in August 2026.

What kind of work do I do?

I do end to end product design including user research, usability testing, wireframing, prototyping, visual design, and accessibility audits. I have worked on B2B platforms, AI tools, fintech, productivity apps, and political campaigns.

Why did I pursue design?

I studied psychology because I wanted to understand people. But I realized I was more interested in how systems shape behavior than individual behavior alone. Design gave me a way to stop studying problems and start solving them. I wanted to build tools that leave an impact in people's lives

What am I currently learning?

I am learning how to use AI in my design process without losing the human element. I am also building prototypes in Swift to test my own ideas, and exploring motion design with Rive to make interactions feel more alive.

Where am I based and am I open to work?

I am based in San Francisco. I am open to full-time, part-time, contract, and remote roles. I am also open to relocating for the right opportunity.
